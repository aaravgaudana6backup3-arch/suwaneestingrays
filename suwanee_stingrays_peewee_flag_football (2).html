About Us

We’re a group of students from the Suwanee area who love football and want to share that excitement with younger kids in our community. Since our school doesn’t have a football team, we created this program to give kids ages 8–11 (flexible based on interest) a chance to be active, have fun, and learn the game in a positive environment.

This is a co-ed flag football team with minimal contact and a strong focus on safety, learning, and enjoyment.

Program Overview

  • Age group: 8–11 (flexible)
  • Co-ed flag football
  • Practices begin Fall 2026
  • Target Spring 2027 8-week league
  • Primary location: Suwanee area

Safety First

Flag Football

No tackling, blocking, or heavy contact.

Supervision

Adult supervision will always be present.

Positive Coaching

Focus on encouragement, learning, and confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this competitive?
It will be structured, but development and fun are the priority.

Do kids need experience?
No experience required. Beginners are welcome.

Is this affiliated with a school?
No. This is an independent, student-led community program.
